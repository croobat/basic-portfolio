About Me

Hello, I'm Luis Antonio Ramírez García, a mechatronics engineer with a passion for programming, particularly web development. With my unique background in engineering and web development skills, I bring a diverse perspective to the projects I work on.

My education in mechatronics engineering has given me a strong foundation in the fundamentals of engineering, including mathematics, physics, mechanics, and electronics. I also developed skills in computer-aided design, simulation, and programming. Throughout my academic career, I consistently sought out opportunities to develop my web development skills, which led me to work on several web-based projects.

In my professional career, I have worked on a variety of web development projects, ranging from small personal websites to large-scale e-commerce platforms. I have expertise in several technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and Python, and I'm experienced in developing responsive and user-friendly web applications, particularly through the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP) and the MERN/MEVN stack (MongoDB, Express, React/Vue and Node). With my engineering background and web development skills, I bring a unique perspective to every project I work on.

When I'm not working on my next project, you can find me tinkering with new gadgets or playing video games. I'm always up for a good challenge, and I enjoy pushing myself to learn and grow in my field.

I'm a firm believer in discipline and constant self-improvement, and I strive to be ethical and responsible in everything I do. If you're looking for a passionate and dedicated engineer who loves what he does, you've come to the right place. Let's work together to create something amazing!
